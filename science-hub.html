🔍 The Scientific Method

Systematic approach to discovery and understanding

Steps:
  1. Observation: Notice something interesting
  2. Question: Ask why or how it happens
  3. Hypothesis: Make an educated guess
  4. Experiment: Test your hypothesis
  5. Conclusion: Analyze results and learn
